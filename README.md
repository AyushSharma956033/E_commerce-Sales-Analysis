E-commerce Sales Analysis
## Project Overview ##
Analyzed e-commerce sales data using **Advanced Excel** and **Power BI** to uncover trends, track KPIs, and generate actionable business insights.

## Tools Used ##
* Advanced Excel (Pivot Tables, INDEX-MATCH, SUMIFS, Data Cleaning)
* Power BI (DAX, Data Modeling, Interactive Dashboards)

## Objectives ##
Analyze overall sales performance
Identify top-performing products and categories
Understand customer purchasing behavior
Track regional sales distribution
Monitor key KPIs such as revenue, profit, and order volume

## Key Analysis Performed ##
1. Sales Performance Analysis
  Total Revenue Generated
  Monthly & Yearly Sales Trends
  Sales Growth Rate
2. Product Analysis
   Top-selling Products
   Most Profitable Categories
    Low-performing Products
3. Customer Insights
    Repeat vs New Customers
    High-value Customers
    Customer Purchase Patterns
4. Regional Analysis
    Sales by Region
    Region-wise Profitability
     Top-performing Locations

## Key Highlights ##
* Identified top-performing products and regions
* Analyzed sales trends and customer behavior
* Built dynamic dashboards with filters and drill-down features
* Tracked KPIs: Revenue, Profit, Orders

## Outcome ##
Delivered insights to support better decision-making in sales strategy, inventory planning, and customer targeting.
